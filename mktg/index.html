Marketers

Quick tips

Company terminology

  • Don’t shorten Aperture Science’s product names.

Grammar

  • Use the active voice whenever possible.

  • Use the singular they as a gender-neutral pronoun.

  • Use standard American English spelling conventions.

  • Use the present tense whenever possible.

Punctuation

  • Use serial commas.

Tone and content

  • Strive for an approachable, friendly tone.

  • Write for a broad audience, not just for experts.

  • Avoid filler words.

  • Try to use short, simple words instead of complex, abstract words.

Formatting and organization

  • Put captions after their associated image.

  • Use descriptive link text.

  • Use title case for titles.

  • Use sentence case for headings.
